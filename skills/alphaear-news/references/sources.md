## Polymarket

- **Base URL**: `https://gamma-api.polymarket.com`
- **Data**: Global prediction markets (e.g., "Will Fed cut rates?", "Who wins the next election?").
- **Usage**: Use `get_active_markets` to retrieve top active markets by volume.
